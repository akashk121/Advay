Reviving the Thrill: Advay'23 Logo Launch and Film Crew Visit

  • byAdmin
  • 20 April 2023

A techno-cultural event named Advay was conducted at a college with a logo launch, film crew visit, dance performance by students, and energetic music by the college band.


Advay 2023: The Unstoppable Techno-Cultural Fest

The much-awaited annual techno-cultural fest of our college, Advay, kickstarted with a bang on April 20th, with the launch of its new logo. Despite the challenges posed by the pandemic, the event has returned with a myriad of exciting events and surprises. The opening day of the fest was nothing short of spectacular, featuring a visit from the film crew of the blockbuster movie Anuragam, who added to the excitement by joining in on the fun.

The program commenced with an electrifying dance performance by our talented students, which left the audience spellbound. But what came next was a complete surprise as the lead actor and actress of Anuragam, Aswin and Gouri respectively, took to the stage and showcased their dance moves along with the students, much to the delight of everyone present. It was an unforgettable moment that truly captured the spirit of Advay.

To top it all off, our college band took the stage and treated us to some high-energy music that had everyone on their feet. The entire day was an unforgettable experience, and the perfect start to what promises to be an exciting event. With more events lined up for the coming days, Advay 2023 is set to be an unstoppable force, bringing together talent and creativity from all corners of our college.
